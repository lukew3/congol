Rules

Congol is based off of John Conway's Game of Life. All of the original rules of Conway's Game of Life are kept in Congol. The original rules are as follows:

  1. Survival: A cell surrounded by two or three cells survives for the next generation
  2. Death: A cell with 4 or more neighbors dies of overpopulation. A cell with one or no neighbors dies of isolation.
  3. Birth: An unoccupied square becomes a living cell if it has exactly 3 neighbors

To make this game playable with multiple species of cells, another rule was added:

The species that contributes the most to the birth of a new cell decides the species of the new cell.

As the game develops, rules will be formed concerning how the game should be played, but the rules stated previously shall always apply
